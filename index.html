01 · About

Curious by default, deliberate by practice.

I'm a third-year Computer Science (AI/ML) student at Sahyadri College of Engineering & Management, currently maintaining a 8.96/10 CGPA. 03 · Projects

Selected works.

Three projects, three different problems — offline multimodal AI, accessible education, and audio-first news consumption.

ACTIVE — FEB 2026
Feb 2026 — Present

VISTA — Offline Multimodal AI Assistant

A 100% offline multimodal assistant: LLaMA 3.2 handles local reasoning and orchestration with zero cloud dependency. BLIP-2 and YOLOv8 are fused for real-time visual captioning and object detection, and Whisper STT + Piper TTS enable fluid, hands-free voice interaction — entirely on local hardware.

Edu Ease — Accessible Learning Platform

A full-stack web app supporting students with visual and auditory impairments — placed Top 30 at Vedha Hackathon. Improved accessibility across 3+ impairment types via TTS, STT, and visual aids, with adaptive, gamified learning paths and multilingual, culturally sensitive content.

Python Flask HTML/CSS JS TTS

News2Voice — Newspaper-to-Audio Converter

A web app that converts news articles and PDFs into audio, using Google Cloud Vision API for OCR and gTTS for speech. Added multilingual support and OpenCV preprocessing to automate the full OCR-to-audio pipeline for visually impaired and non-literate users.
